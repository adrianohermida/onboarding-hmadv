# LGPD Foundation

## Scope

- consent tracking and revocation
- terms versioning
- treatment purpose tracking
- retention policy definitions
- future-ready export/anonymization/deletion architecture

## Mandatory Consent Metadata

- user_id
- tenant_id
- consent_type
- consent_version
- accepted_at
- revoked_at
- ip_address
- user_agent
- legal_basis
- treatment_purpose
